F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: How do I use the dropshipper app to browse and select products?

    A: Using our dropshipper app is simple! Once you sign in, you can navigate through various categories and collections to browse the available clothes. You can apply filters such as price range, size, color, and style to narrow down your search. Once you find a product you like, you can view its details, including product images, descriptions, and specifications.

  • Q: How do I place an order through the dropshipper app?

    A: To place an order, select the desired product and add it to your cart. Proceed to the checkout page, where you can review your order, provide shipping information, and select the preferred payment method. After confirming the order, you will receive an order confirmation with the relevant details.

  • Q: Can I track my orders through the dropshipper app?

    A: Absolutely! Our dropshipper app offers order tracking functionality. Once your order is confirmed, you will receive a tracking number. You can enter this tracking number in the app to monitor the status of your shipment, including estimated delivery dates and real-time tracking updates.

  • Q: How can I keep my products in top ranking as a supplier?

    A: Ensure your products stay in top rankings by adding relevant keywords to optimize their visibility. This helps potential customers find your products more easily and increases the chances of higher rankings in search results.

  • Q: How can I become a supplier for the dropshipper app?

    A: To become a supplier, log in or create an account, fill out your profile with accurate details, and wait for activation within 24 hours.

  • Q: What are the requirements to become a dropshipper for the app?

    A: Becoming a dropshipper for our app is simple. We welcome individuals and businesses to join our dropshipping program. The requirements include having an active online presence, a valid business license (if applicable), and the ability to promote and sell products effectively. Once you meet these requirements, you can sign up through the app or website and start dropshipping immediately.
